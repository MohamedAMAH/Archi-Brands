Who We Are

Archi-brands is a leading supplier in Egypt of innovative architectural solutions, offering a range of sustainable, functional, and aesthetic products for various building types. They provide products such as engineered wood, vinyl/LVT, and carpet tiles, with a focus on comfort, quality, and environmental responsibility. They are exclusive agents for major manufacturers like Shaw Contract and Gerflor.
