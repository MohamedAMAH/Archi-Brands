WHAT WE DO?

Archi-brands is Egypt's premier supplier of architectural solutions, delivering aesthetic, functional, and durable solutions that redefine spaces with premium quality and innovation. Our extensive range of products includes acoustic comfort options, carpet tiles, vinyl/LVT, engineered wood, and more.

Our commitment to being environmentally friendly is central to our mission. As the sole agents of leading manufacturers like Shaw Contract, Impact Acoustic, SAS International, and Gerflor, we provide solutions that not only meet your needs but also contribute to a sustainable future.

We offer a full range of services from interior design and space planning to procurement and installation of:

  • Furniture
  • Flooring
  • Ceiling
  • Cladding

We uphold passion, integrity, teamwork, and accountability as the core values driving our work, ensuring each project is executed with excellence and reliability.
